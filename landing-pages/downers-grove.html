We Know This Village

Roofing Built for Downers Grove Homes

Founded in 1832 and proud to be the "Village of Volunteers," Downers Grove blends century-old craftsmanship with neighborhoods that expect things done right. We get it — and we roof to that standard.

  • Historic district expertise. Steep-slope Victorians near downtown and the Tivoli need flashing and ventilation done by hand — exactly how our crews work.
  • Built for our climate. DuPage County freeze-thaw swings and ice dams off the lake punish weak roofs. We spec ice-and-water shield and ventilation to match.
  • From Hobson to Belmont. We've handled tear-offs and repairs across the village — and near the Morton Arboretum tree line where debris and shade demand smart material choices.
  • Trusted by the Downers Grove Golf Club crowd. The folks who keep one of America's oldest golf clubs (est. 1892) immaculate tend to want their roof handled the same way. So do we.
